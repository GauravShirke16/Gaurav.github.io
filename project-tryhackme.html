Overview

TryHackMe is a browser-based cybersecurity training platform where you complete hands-on "rooms" — guided challenges covering everything from Linux basics to advanced penetration testing. Each room I complete will be documented here with a full write-up: the approach, the tools used, the commands run, and lessons learned.

Write-ups are valuable because they prove you didn't just click through — they show you understood the technique well enough to explain it. Recruiters and hiring managers actively look for candidate write-ups on GitHub and personal blogs.
